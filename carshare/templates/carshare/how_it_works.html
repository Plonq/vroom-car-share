Four simple steps and Vroom will have you on the road in no time.

Step 1

Register

Begin by registering with us for free. We’ll request some basic information to confirm your eligibility, and then send out your VroomCard for delivery on the next business day.

Image here

Step 2

Make a Booking

Use the interactive map to search for available vehicles in your chosen location. Select the one that’s right for you and make a booking for the time period you need. Your booking is available to view in your profile, and you can cancel any time in advance of the booking start time.

Image here

Step 3

Collect Vehicle

Simply use your VroomCard to unlock your booked vehicle. The smart reader links your card with your booking, allowing you access to the vehicle. The keys are inside and you’re on your way!

Image here

Step 4

Return and Pay

Return the car to its home and end your booking. Your nominated credit card will be charged, and you will receive a tax invoice by email. You can view your full booking history, as well as any upcoming bookings, in your profile.
